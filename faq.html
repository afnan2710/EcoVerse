Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about trees, plants, and Ecoverse services

Your Questions Answered

What types of trees can I purchase from Ecoverse?

At Ecoverse, we offer a wide range of trees, including ancient, rare, and native species that are ethically sourced and suitable for a variety of climates.

How are the plants shipped to my location?

We take great care in packaging our plants. They are carefully wrapped in eco-friendly materials and shipped in specially designed boxes to ensure safe and secure delivery.

Can I return a plant if it arrives damaged?

Yes, we have a return policy in place. If your plant arrives damaged, you can contact our customer service team within 7 days for a replacement or refund.

What kind of care do the plants need after delivery?

We provide detailed care instructions with each plant to ensure it thrives in its new environment. This includes guidelines on watering, sunlight, and soil preferences.

Do you offer bulk orders for landscaping projects?

Yes, we offer bulk orders for both residential and commercial landscaping projects. Contact our sales team for pricing and availability on larger quantities.
